# Excel-Dashboards

Excel dashboards and analysis using pivot tables, formulas, and data visualization.

## Bike Sales Customer Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing bike purchase behavior across 1,026 customers, exploring how demographics, income, region, and lifestyle factors influence buying decisions.

**Key metrics tracked:** Total Bikes Purchased (495), Total Customers (1,026), Average Income ($56,208.58)

## Process
- Cleaned raw customer data (handled inconsistent entries, formatted currency and categorical fields)
- Built pivot tables to summarize purchases by gender, age group, income level, region, and commute distance
- Designed an interactive dashboard using slicers for Marital Status, Gender, Education, Region, Income Level, and Age Group
- Created charts to visualize purchase trends: bar charts, line charts, and pie charts

## Key Insights
- **Age:** Middle-aged customers are the most likely to purchase bikes (658 purchases), far ahead of Old (283) and Young (85) age groups.
- **Income:** Medium-income earners purchase the most bikes (262), while High-income earners purchase the least (113) — despite having more spending power.
- **Region:** North America leads in total purchases (220), followed by Europe (156) and the Pacific (119).
- **Commute distance:** Customers with a 0–1 mile commute purchased the most bikes (207), suggesting proximity to work may drive purchase decisions more than long-distance commuting needs.
- **Gender:** Purchases are nearly even between Male (525) and Female (501) customers.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing on Medium-income earners, since they contribute the highest sales volume.
- Target working-class adults and families, since major purchases come from the middle-age demographic.
- Attract younger buyers with student discounts, installment payment plans, and social media campaigns.
- In the Pacific region, invest in market research to better understand customer needs and improve product accessibility and pricing.
- In Europe, maintain steady marketing efforts and introduce region-specific bike models.
- Expand investment in North America through loyalty programs, given the region's high demand.
